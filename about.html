About Me

DevOps Engineer focused on reproducible delivery, observability, and secure configurations

With hands-on experience shipping CI/CD to cloud targets and instrumenting services for measurable outcomes, I specialize in building systems that are reliable, scalable, and maintainable.

My Approach

I emphasize Git-driven pipelines, Infrastructure as Code reproducibility, cloud-agnostic patterns, and on-demand demos over persistent, costly environments. Every system I build is designed with observability, security, and cost-efficiency in mind.

Core Principles

  • Reproducibility First: Every deployment should be repeatable and version-controlled
  • Observe Everything: Metrics, logs, and traces tell the story of system health
  • Security by Design: Security considerations integrated throughout the pipeline
  • Cost-Conscious: Efficient resource utilization and on-demand provisioning
